About

Hi I'm Christian and I’ve always been captivated by the power of storytelling. Whether it’s a complex narrative or a simple idea, stories are at the heart of the human experience—they inspire everything we do.

After studying Film and TV Production at the University of York, I spent nine years working in post-production for broadcast TV while also running my own film projects. Along the way, I’ve honed a diverse range of skills, knowledge and experience needed to tell compelling stories through filmmaking.

The rapid development of technology in the audio-visual space has greatly empowered a solo filmmaker to create high-quality, impactful content. With abilities end to end in video creation I now am looking to offer my services to help bring your story to life - or even help you discover it.
