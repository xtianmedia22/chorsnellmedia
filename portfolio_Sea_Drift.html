Alan Young Introduces "Sea-Drift"

A film, over an hour long, commissioned by the Delius society and written by Alan Young for which I planned, shot, and completed all post production for illustative and interveiw footage to support the story.

The film tells the life story of the Bradford born composer Frederick Delius through interview with the author Alan Young and excerpts from his recently published novel "Sea-Drift" which is a novelization of Delius' life.

The project required me to coordinate with both the society and Alan as well as find ways to visually illustrate the book chapters with no cast or props beyond what I could source myself. I then needed to plan shoots to acquire the material and bring the film together in post production.
